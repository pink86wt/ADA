Important update regarding the start of the 2020/2021 Academic year

Last updated 3 mins ago

The Apple Developer Academy must put safety and well-being of our students, mentors and staff first and foremost. In light of the global second wave of the COVID-19 pandemic, we have decided to postpone the start of the coming Academic year. We are hoping that we will be able to welcome all selected students in person in September 2021.

More information will follow via our official website FAQ page and social media channels. If you have any urgent questions please contact us at developeracademy@unina.it.
